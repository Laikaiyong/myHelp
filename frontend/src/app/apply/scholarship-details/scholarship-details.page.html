ABOUT THE SCHOLARSHIP

Are you looking to boost your tech potential in an inclusive and diverse workforce? This scholarship is aimed at celebrating and supporting young women with big ambitions for careers in science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM). We want to enable young women from all backgrounds to become leaders in the different tech fields including computer science, engineering and technical studies.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE?

This scholarship is open to outstanding Malaysian women intending to pursue their undergraduate studies in the following fields of study: 1.Engineering (Network/Telecommunication/Software/Mechanical/Electrical/Electronic) 2.Computer Science 3.Data Science/Actuarial Science 4.Mathematics/Statistics/Applied Sciences
